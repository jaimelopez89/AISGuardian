# Elisa FEC Cable Sabotage Investigation

**Date:** December 31, 2025
**Location:** Gulf of Finland / Porkkala Area
**Incident Type:** Suspected Anchor Drag (similar to Eagle S incident Dec 2024)
**Status:** ACTIVE INVESTIGATION

---

## Incident Summary

On the morning of December 31, 2025, Elisa Corporation's FEC-1 and FEC-2 submarine fiber optic cables connecting Helsinki, Finland to Tallinn, Estonia were damaged. The incident occurred in the Gulf of Finland near the Porkkala Peninsula, approximately 30km west of Helsinki.

This incident follows a similar pattern to the December 25, 2024 cable sabotage where the tanker Eagle S allegedly dragged its anchor ~100km across the seabed, severing multiple cables including Estlink 2.
